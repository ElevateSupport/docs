<Accordion title="ERLC Moderator Role" icon="hammer" iconType="regular">
  `/erlc hint` - Send a **:h** hint into the in-game server

  `/erlc players` - View the current in-game players, callsigns and teams

  `/erlc killlogs` - View past kill logs

  `/erlc joinlogs` - View past join and leave logs

  `/erlc queue` - View the current server queue

  `/erlc vehicles` - View current spawned vehicles including teams, players and vehicle models

  `/erlc info` - Sends a general embed with an auto updating live player count

  `/erlc commandlogs` - View the past command logs, including who ran the command

  `/shift manage` - Manage their own shift

  `/shift active` - View all staff members actively on shift

  `/shift leaderboard` - View the per-server shift leaderboard

  ### In-game Commands

  `:log test` - A quick test of the custom command system

  `:log shift start` - Starts the users shift

  `:log shift break` - Go on break

  `:log shift resume` - Resume their shift, stop their break

  `:log shift end` - Ends the users shift and shows them the length of the shift
</Accordion>
