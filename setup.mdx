title: "Setup"
---

Setting up Elevate is simpler than ever with our efficent panel.

<Warning>
  Only members with the \*\*Administrator \*\*permission are authorized to access the `/setup` command, which means they can adjust \*\*any \*\*of your servers configuration.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="ER:LC" stepNumber={1} titleSize="h2">
    This option is where you can go to set your ER:LC API Key **(case sensitive)** and also setup other options involving your ER:LC server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permissions" titleSize="h2">
    You can configure the roles to manage what each member may access within your server.

    <Info>
      You can view more information on permissions in [Permissions](https://docs.elevate.nclrp.xyz/misc/permissions)
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
